What is the hardwood furniture?

Hardwood furniture is made from deciduous trees (like oak, maple, and walnut) and is generally denser, more durable, and more expensive.

what is the sofa?

What is modular furniture?

Modular furniture consists of individual pieces that can be combined in various ways to create different configurations.
